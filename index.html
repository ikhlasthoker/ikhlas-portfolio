About Me

Passionate learner. Creative thinker. Problem solver.

I'm a self-taught developer who grew up experimenting on a single Android phone long before ever touching a full desktop setup — and that constraint became my superpower. Working entirely from Termux, AIDE and mobile tooling taught me to be resourceful, curious and relentless about figuring things out.

Today I build across the stack — Android apps, Telegram bots, full web platforms, UI systems, graphics and edited video — and I'm increasingly obsessed with AI automation: teaching software to do the repetitive work so people can focus on what matters.

I love modern technologies, I'm always improving my skills, and I treat every bug as a puzzle rather than a wall. Clean design and efficient code aren't separate goals for me — they're the same goal.
